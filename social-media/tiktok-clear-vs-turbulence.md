# TikTok — "Same flight. Two skies." (turbulence vs. clear skies)

The most ownable Naviio concept: a pure visual metaphor. Same plane, same
destination — one flight is violent turbulence (no Naviio), the other is calm
clear skies (Naviio). The viewer *feels* the difference before reading a word.

**Length:** 15–22s · 9:16 · loopable · the split happens at the midpoint.

---

The drop / breakthrough at ~8s is the whole video — cut your audio so the beat
lands exactly when the clouds part.

## How to actually make it (3 paths, fastest first)

### Path A — screen-record the animated preview (5 min, free)
The looping scene I built in chat IS this video in motion. Screen-record it
(QuickTime on Mac, or CapCut's screen record), crop to 9:16, add a Commercial-
Library track with the drop at the cloud-break, and post. Roughest but instant.

### Path B — AI-generated cinematic footage (best look, ~30 min)
Generate the two hero shots with a text-to-video tool (Runway Gen-3, Pika, Sora,
Kling, Luma) and cut them together with the breakthrough as the transition.
Paste these prompts (all 9:16, ~5s each):

> **Turbulence shot:** "Cinematic POV from an airplane window during violent
> turbulence at night. Dark storm clouds, lightning flashes, rain streaking
> across the glass, the wing visibly shaking, heavy camera shake, tense ominous
> mood, moody blue-grey palette, photorealistic, vertical 9:16."

> **Breakthrough shot:** "Cinematic POV from an airplane window punching up
> through thick clouds into calm clear blue sky at golden sunrise, smooth steady
> flight, soft sun flare, serene and hopeful mood, teal and blue palette, gentle
> upward tilt, photorealistic, vertical 9:16."

> **Optional cruise shot:** "Airplane wing gliding over a calm sea of clouds at
> sunrise, perfectly smooth, peaceful, cinematic, vertical 9:16."

Then overlay the hexagon HUD (`graphics/hexagon-score.svg`) and end card
(`graphics/cta-end-card.svg`) in CapCut.

### Path C — stock b-roll (reliable, ~20 min)
Pull two clips from Pexels/Storyblocks: "airplane turbulence storm window" and
"airplane above clouds sunrise." Cut on the cloud-break, add the overlays + text.
