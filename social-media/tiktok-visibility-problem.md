# TikTok — "Flying Blind" (from the Visibility Problem post)

**Goal:** stop the scroll, create a curiosity gap, resolve it with a satisfying
visual, end on the waitlist. ~25s, sound-off friendly, loopable.

**Format:** text-on-screen over b-roll (no need to be on camera). A talking-head
variant is at the bottom if you want to show your face (often outperforms for B2B).

---

## Why this is built to travel (the mechanics)
- **1.5-second hook** that contradicts an assumption ("it's not revenue") →
  pattern interrupt + curiosity gap.
- **Relatable fear** (running out of cash without seeing it coming) — fear/relief
  is the most shareable emotion for founders.
- **Fast cuts** every 1.5–2.5s and constant motion = higher average watch time.
- **Satisfying resolve**: visual chaos (numbers everywhere) collapses into ONE
  clean score. "Chaos → order" is inherently rewatchable.
- **Sound-off design**: the story works with captions only (80% watch muted).
- **Loop**: the last frame flows back into the first line.
- **Comment bait** in the caption (a question) to spike engagement early.

## On-screen text rules (for retention)
- One short line at a time, large, high-contrast white on the navy.
- Keep each line on screen ≤2.5s; never more than ~6 words.
- Use TikTok's auto-captions style (the platform boosts captioned videos).
- First line must be readable within 0.5s — no animation delay on the hook.

## Audio
- ⚠️ **Business/creator accounts can only use TikTok's Commercial Music Library**
  (not viral pop songs) without takedown risk. Pick a **tense-build-that-drops**
  track from the Commercial Library, or trending sounds available to your account.
- The "drop" must land at **~11s** (the hexagon reveal). Cut your visuals to the beat.
- If you film a talking-head, keep your VO + a low instrumental bed.

## Editing
- CapCut: 9:16, 1080×1920. Use "zoom punch" transitions on the hook, hard cuts
  for the montage, a smooth "scale-in" for the hexagon reveal.
- Color: lean into the navy/blue + teal brand. Add a subtle film grain + light
  vignette so screen recordings look intentional.

## Posting playbook
- Post 1 video, then **reply to your top comments with a second video** (the
  algorithm rewards reply-videos and it doubles your shots on goal).
- Best windows for B2B/founder audiences: Tue–Thu, ~8–10am or ~7–9pm local.
- Reply to every comment in the first 60 minutes (early engagement = reach).
- Repurpose the exact same export to Instagram Reels and YouTube Shorts.

## Talking-head variant (often best for B2B)
Film yourself, fast and casual, captions burned in:
- (0–2s) "Most businesses don't fail because of revenue."
- (2–6s) "They fail because nobody saw the cash run out — until it was gone."
- (6–12s) "Your bank, your books, and Stripe never agree, so you're guessing."
- (12–18s) "I'm building Naviio so founders get one real-time score instead of three dashboards that lie to each other."
- (18–22s) "Six metrics. One number. Updated live."
- (22–25s) "It launches Q4 2026 — link's in my bio to get on the waitlist."
