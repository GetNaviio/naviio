# Naviio TikTok Series — "Flying Blind" (5 concepts)

A connected series off the "visibility problem" theme. All ~20–30s, 9:16,
sound-off friendly, loopable. Each leans into Naviio's aviation identity so the
content is *ownable*, not generic fintech.

**Shared rules:** big captions, ≤6 words on screen at a time, cut to the beat,
end on "join the waitlist." Business/creator accounts: use TikTok's **Commercial
Music Library** only. Repurpose every export to Reels + Shorts. Reply to top
comments with follow-up videos.

|------|--------|----------------|-------|
