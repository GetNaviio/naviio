# Benchmarks Pilot — Recruiting Messages

**Goal:** recruit 25 design-partner customers for the peer-benchmarks pilot.
**Who:** seed/Series-A founders and finance leads who already run on Stripe + a connected bank (the data Naviio needs to benchmark them).
**The ask:** connect their accounts, use Naviio free during the pilot, and give us feedback. **The offer:** they see how their spend and vendor pricing compares to similar-size companies — something they can't get anywhere else.

All copy is privacy-first by construction: benchmarks are anonymized aggregates only, never any single company's numbers, and a peer figure only shows when at least 5 comparable businesses are in the pool.

---

## 1. Cold email

**Subject lines (pick one / A-B test):**
- Are you overpaying for your SaaS vs. companies your size?
- 25 founders, one question: how does your burn compare?
- A benchmark you actually can't get anywhere else

**Body:**

Hi {First name},

Quick one. We're opening a 25-company pilot for Naviio's peer benchmarks, and {company} looks like a fit.

Every founder I talk to asks the same thing — *"is this normal?"* Is our AWS bill high for our size? Are we spending too much on tools? Is our burn in line with companies like us? Today the only way to answer that is to ask around and hope someone's honest.

Naviio answers it from your actual numbers. Connect Stripe and your bank, and within minutes you see how your spend per category, your vendor pricing, and your burn compare to anonymized companies in your revenue band. No spreadsheets, no survey, no consultant.

What the pilot involves:
- Connect your accounts (read-only; takes ~5 minutes)
- Use Naviio free for the duration of the pilot
- A 20-minute feedback call so we build the right thing

In return you get the benchmarks, the full dashboard, and direct line to us. Everything you see about peers is anonymized and aggregated — we only ever show a peer number when at least 5 comparable companies are in the pool, and your data is never shown to anyone else.

Open to it? Reply "in" and I'll send a link.

{Your name}
Naviio

*P.S. — read-only connection, and you can disconnect and delete your data anytime from settings.*

## Sending notes (internal)

- **Sequencing:** warm intros and founder Slacks first (highest reply rate), cold email last.
- **Qualify before sending:** they need Stripe + a connectable bank, otherwise benchmarks are empty for them.
- **Cap at 25** so feedback calls stay manageable; keep a short overflow waitlist.
- **Track** source (intro / Slack / cold) so we know which channel converts.
- **Honesty guardrail:** never imply we show a specific competitor's numbers — the pitch is aggregates only. That privacy promise is the product.
